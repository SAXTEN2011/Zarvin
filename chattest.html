Here you can manage the various BetterTTV options. Click On or Off to toggle settings.

Anon Chat  —  Join channels without appearing in chat
Alpha Chat Badges  —  Removes the background from chat badges
Automatic Theatre Mode  —  Automatically enables theatre mode
BetterTTV Emotes  —  BetterTTV adds extra cool emotes for you to use
BetterTTV GIF Emotes  —  We realize not everyone likes GIFs, but some people do.
Blue Buttons  —  Blue is better than purple, so we make it an option.
Chat Image Preview  —  Preview chat images on mouse over
DarkenTTV  —  A sleek, grey theme which will make you love the site even more
Default to Live Channels  —  BetterTTV can click on "Channels" for you in the Following Overview automatically
Desktop Notifications  —  BetterTTV can send you desktop notifications when you are tabbed out of Twitch
Double-Click Translation  —  Double-clicking on chat lines translates them with Google Translate
Directory Preview  —  Hover over streams to get a live preview of the stream
Disable Host Mode  —  Disables hosted channels on Twitch
Disable Name Colors  —  Disables colors in chat (useful for those who may suffer from color blindness)
Disable Whispers  —  Disables the Twitch whisper feature and hides any whispers you receive
Double-Click Auto-Complete  —  Double-clicking a username in chat copies it into the chat text box
Embedded Polling  —  See polls posted by the broadcaster embedded right into chat
Emote Menu  —  Get a more advanced emote menu for Twitch. (Made by Ryan Chatham)
Featured Channels  —  The left sidebar is too cluttered, so BetterTTV removes featured channels by default
Following Notifications  —  BetterTTV will notify you when channels you follow go live
Hide Group Chat  —  Hides the group chat bar above chat
Hide Spam Messages  —  Hides known spam messages. Click on the message to reveal it
Host Button  —  Places a Host/Unhost button below the video player
JTV Chat Badges  —  BetterTTV can replace the chat badges with the ones from JTV
JTV Monkey Emotes  —  BetterTTV replaces the robot emoticons with the old JTV monkey faces
Mod Card Keybinds  —  Enable keybinds when you click on a username: P(urge), T(imeout), B(an), W(hisper)
Other Messages Alert  —  BetterTTV can alert you when you receive a message to your "Other" messages folder
Pin Highlighted Messages  —  Pin your ten latest highlighted messages right above chat
Play Sound on Highlight/Whisper  —  Get audio feedback for messages directed at you (BETA)
Remove Deleted Messages  —  Completely removes timed out messages from view
Shift-Click Custom Timeouts  —  Requires shift + click to activate the custom timeout selector
Show Deleted Messages  —  Turn this on to change <message deleted> back to users' messages.
Split Chat  —  Easily distinguish between messages from different users in chat
Tab Completion Tooltip  —  Shows a tooltip with suggested names when using tab completion
Timeout Pinned Highlights  —  Automatically hide pinned highlights after 1 minute
Unread Whispers Count in Title  —  Display the number of unread whispers in the tab title

Our Privacy Policy was last updated and posted on November 4, 2013. It governs the privacy terms of our Addon, located at http://www.nightdev.com/betterttv.

Your Privacy

Definitions
"Non Personal Information" is information that is not personally identifiable to you and that we automatically collect when you access our Addon with a web browser. It may also include publicly available information that is shared between you and others.

"Personally Identifiable Information" is non-public information that is personally identifiable to you and obtained in order for us to provide you within our Addon. Personally Identifiable Information may include information such as your email address, and other related information that you provide to us or that we obtain about you.

Information We Collect
Generally, you control the amount and type of information you provide to us when using our Addon.

You are not required to provide us with any Personally Identifiable Information as a regular Visitor. Should you sign into any parts of the addon, you share Personally Identifiable Information with us.

Computer Information Collected
When you use our Addon, we automatically collect certain computer information by the interaction of your web browser with our Addon. Such information is typically considered Non Personal Information. We collect the following:

  • LocalStorage
    Our Addon uses "LocalStorage" to save user settings within the Addon. LocalStorage is similar to a Cookie, which is a small piece of data stored on your computer by your web browser. We use LocalStorage to personalize the Content that you see on our Addon. Most web browsers can be set to disable the use of LocalStorage. However, if you disable LocalStorage, you may not be able to access functionality on our Addon correctly or at all. We never place Personally Identifiable Information in LocalStorage.

  • Automatic Information
    We automatically receive information from your web browser through the Addon. This information mainly includes an Event that was triggered inside the Addon (such as a disconnection from a chat server). This information also includes the IP address of your computer/proxy server that you use to access the Internet, your Internet Service Provider name, web browser type, and computer operating system. We use all of this information to analyze trends among our Users to help improve our Addon and to also provide realtime, completely anonymous statistics.

  • Customizing Our Addon

  • Sharing Information with Affiliates and Other Third Parties

We may provide your Non Personally Identifiable Information to affiliates that provide services to us with regards to our Addon (i.e. statistical research, etc.); such affiliates will only receive information necessary to provide the respective services and will be bound by confidentiality agreements limiting the use of such information.

  • Data Aggregation
    We retain the right to collect and use any Non Personal Information collected from your use of our Addon and aggregate such data for internal analytics that improve our Addon and Service as well as for use or resale to others. At no time is your Personally Identifiable Information included in such data aggregations.

  • Legally Required Releases of Information
    We may be legally required to disclose your Personally Identifiable Information, if such disclosure is (a) required by subpoena, law, or other legal process; (b) necessary to assist law enforcement officials or government enforcement agencies; (c) necessary to investigate violations of or otherwise enforce our Legal Terms; (d) necessary to protect us from legal action or claims from third parties including you and/or other Members; and/or (e) necessary to protect the legal rights, personal/real property, or personal safety of BetterTTV, our Users, employees, and affiliates.
